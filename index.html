ABOUT ME

I am a front-end developer with a background in graphic design freelancing field. My nearly 10 years design experience has given me an extra bonus for web development and interaction design, allow me to make sure no details are lost in translation.

Building aesthetic tech solutions is my biggest passion, and coding has been a life-long hobby for me. When I was young, I became obsessed with the archaic blog, called - Xanga, and spent a few years building skin/theme as a self-taught HTML&CSS amateur. During Covid, I would like to step outside of my comfort zone, so I decided to pick up coding again, then pursued a formal education at Code Youth.

Other than coding, I love reading, taking walk with my husky - Zombie to meeting our friends and playing volleyball.
